Welcome to The Church

Alpha

Want to know more about God?

The Alpha course is a 10 week series of interactive sessions where people are able to explore the Christian faith and are free to ask any questions they may have. We meet together in our church for food, a short talk and a discussion to share thoughts and ask questions. This is for anyone who have an interest in Christianity; this is a relaxed and friendly environment. People are encouraged to be honest, debate and explore faith. There is an absolutely no charge involved. If you are interested come along!

Starting 6th May, 7pm in The Sanctuary.
