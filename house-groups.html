Welcome to The Church

House Groups

We as a church believe that meeting together in small groups is such an important part of our Christian faith; house groups are a key part of what we do. House groups give an opportunity for a more relaxed atmosphere in a way that can't be offered during a Sunday service in the same way. We give everyone the opportunity to discuss issues, pray together, worship and read God's word. House groups are a great place to get to know others within the church and people have established really valuable friendships from attending.

We would highly recommend everyone in our congregation to join a house group!

  • The Monday house group meets at Margaret and David's at 7:30pm.
  • The Wednesday house group meets at Caleb and Abbie's house at 7:30pm.
  • There is also a house group on Thursday in the church which meets at 12:00pm.

    • If you would like more information or would like to sign up to one of the house groups feel free to send us an EMAIL.
