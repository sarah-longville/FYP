About Us

We are a church family passionate about bringing the love of God to our community and seeing lives transformed because of Him. We are passionate in encouraging each other in the Christian life and being equipped through Biblical teaching. We want Jesus to affect every day of our lives and believe supporting each other will help to achieve this.

For information about any of the events go to the EVENTS page. You would be so welcome!

Our Values

  • We believe in the trinity; Father, Son and Holy Spirit
  • We believe in a loving God
  • We are devoted to evangelism and living Jesus out in our day to day lives.
  • The crucifixion of Christ has as much significance today as it did 2000 years ago.
  • We believe in baptism by full emersion.
  • God has the power to transform lives.
  • We are called to make a difference

    I want to know more about God

    If you have got questions about faith and want to know more about how God can fit into your life you would be more than welcome to attend our Alpha Course. The Alpha course is a 10 week series of interactive sessions where people are able to explore the Christian faith and are free to ask any questions they may have.
